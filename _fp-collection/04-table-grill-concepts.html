Table Grill Concepts

Internal electrical and thermal components for consumer table grills are standardized and cheap. Value can almost exclusively be created by designing a unique form factor. In these concepts I focussed on designing three distinct form factors by playing with the overal aesthetics of the housing and by thinking of different layouts for the cast aluminium grill surface.
